About

Lil Storm is a rising singer-songwriter and multi-instrumentalist making waves with his signature fusion of electronic vibes and live instrumentation.

Originally from Jalingo, Taraba State, Lil Storm (born Sylvanus Sunday) started writing music at the age of 12 and dropped his first self-produced EP while still in high school—hinting early at a rare creative spark.

His breakout moment came with the 2019 release of *First Light*, a project that caught attention for its raw emotion and genre-blending style, quickly building him a loyal underground following.

Now gaining traction across streaming platforms and indie stages, Lil Storm is becoming one of the most exciting new names in the alt-pop and electronic scene.

His latest album, *Midnight Echoes*, explores love, loss, and self-reflection through dreamy production and deeply personal lyrics—proving he’s not just arriving, he’s here to stay.
